Sim Card Vending KIOSK

The Sim Card Dispensing Kiosk by Smart Group International enables Telecom Companies to enroll new users to their services. These Self-Service SIM Card Kiosks are easy to set up and user-friendly.

You may so wish to install a number of them such to automate SIM Card Distribution and Revenue Collection. This very Kiosk is especially desired in City Centers, Commercial Spaces, Shopping Malls, in addition to Airports.

Features
  • Interactive and Easy-to-Use.
  • Can dispense 4 types of Ready-to-Use SIM Cards.
  • Verification through an ID Card, Passport Scanner, and a Digital Signature.
  • Secure and Encrypted Transactions.
  • Tamperproof Cash Storage.
  • Integration with Magnetic and Chip Card Readers and Printers.
  • Supports Debit/Credit Cards, Account Transfer, and Direct Debit Transfer through one’s mobile.
  • Available in numerous Languages.
